Mohamed M. Kamra

Mohamed graduated from the aerospace department at the faculty of engineering, Cairo University where he was awarded his bachelor of engineering and master of science in 2010 and 2015 respectively. Mohamed traveled to Japan where he obtained his PhD. degree in computational fluid dynamics from Kyushu University in 2018. In the same year, Mohamed a fellowship to study the effect of violent sloshing in LNG tanks with complex internal structure. Later, he managed to secure an assistant professor position in Kyushu University where he has overseen several post graduate  students.
